Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I import my data from QuickBooks?

    You can import your Chart of Accounts and Customers directly from QuickBooks CSV exports. Go to the Accounts or Customer Hub and look for the "Import QuickBooks Data" button.

  • How do I reconcile my bank statements?

    In the Banking section, choose a bank account and click "Reconcile". Follow the step-by-step match wizard to link your bank transactions to your ledger entries.

  • Can I add multiple users to my company?

    Yes, go to Settings > Users to invite team members and assign them roles like Administrator, Accountant, or Viewer.
