Customer Profile Management

Master your client relationships and financial tracking.

Overview

VeroBooks provides a comprehensive 360-degree view of every customer. Beyond simple contact storage, the Customer Profile serves as a financial dashboard for your relationship with each client.

The Financial Pulse

Each profile includes three critical KPIs calculated in real-time:

  • Total Revenue: The sum of all invoices issued to the client (lifetime).
  • Total Collected: The total amount of payments received.
  • Outstanding Balance: The remaining balance across all open and overdue invoices.

Advanced Profile Data

To take your bookkeeping to the next level, you can configure professional-grade fields for each client:

  • Payment Terms: Set defaults like Net 30 or Due on Receipt so new invoices are automatically calculated correctly.
  • Credit Limits: Monitor client exposure and prevent over-extension.
  • Internal Notes: Maintain a secure, internal-only log of billing preferences or history.
  • Default Currency: Ensure invoices are always generated in the client's preferred currency.

Daily Workflows

From the Customer Profile, you can perform high-velocity actions:

Creating Invoices

Click the "Create Invoice" button to jump directly to a pre-filled invoice form for that customer.

Viewing Transactions

Use the tabbed interface to filter between Invoices, Payments, and Appointments.

Pro Tip: CRM Integration

If you have the **Appointments** module enabled, you can see all upcoming and past meetings directly on the customer profile tab, ensuring your billing team is always in sync with the service team.
