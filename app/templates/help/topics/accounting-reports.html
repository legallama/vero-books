Accounting & Reports

Understand your financial position with professional reporting.

The General Ledger

The General Ledger is the backbone of VeroBooks. Every transaction—from a simple invoice to a complex payroll entry—is ultimately recorded as a set of debits and credits in the ledger.

Essential Reports

Profit & Loss (P&L)

Also known as the Income Statement. It shows your revenue minus your expenses over a specific period.

Balance Sheet

A "snapshot" in time showing what you own (Assets), what you owe (Liabilities), and your Equity.

Manual Journal Entries

For adjustments like depreciation, payroll accruals, or opening balance corrections, use the **Journal Entry** tool.

VeroBooks ensures every journal entry is balanced. You cannot post an entry where total debits do not equal total credits.

Audit Log & Accountability

Transparency is key to financial integrity. The **Audit Log** tracks every significant action in the system, including who created an invoice, when a bill was edited, and who performed a bank sync.
