Getting Started with VeroBooks

Welcome! This guide will help you set up your company and start managing your books effectively.

1. Set Up Your Company

The first step is to ensure your company profile is complete. Head over to Settings > Company Profile to upload your logo, set your fiscal year-end, and define your reporting currency.

2. Invite Your Team

Accounting is a team sport. Invite your bookkeeper, accountant, or business partners in Settings > Users. You can control their access levels with granular roles.

3. Connect Your Bank

VeroBooks works best when it's synced with your real-world transactions. Go to Banking and use the "Connect Bank" feature to securely link your accounts.

Pro Tip: Use "Bank Rules" to automatically categorize recurring transactions like rent or utility payments.

4. Create Your First Invoice

Start getting paid! Navigate to Sales & Revenue > Invoices and click "New Invoice". You can customize your invoice templates to match your brand.
