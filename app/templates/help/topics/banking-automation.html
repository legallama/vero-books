Banking & Automation

Save hours every week with automated transaction matching.

The Live Transaction Feed

VeroBooks connects to your bank accounts to provide a live stream of transactions. You can view this in the Banking section.

Transactions in the feed can have several statuses:

  • UNCATEGORIZED: The transaction is new and needs to be matched to an account in your ledger.
  • MATCHED: The transaction has been linked to a journal entry and is reflected in your books.

Bank Rules: The Secret to Speed

Bank Rules allow you to automate the categorization of recurring transactions. For example, you can create a rule that says: "Any transaction containing 'Amazon' should be categorized as 'Office Expenses'."

Types of Rule Logic:

  • Description Matching: Match based on text in the bank's description.
  • Amount Matching: Match specific amounts or ranges.
  • Auto-Post: Enable this to have the system automatically create the journal entry and post it to your ledger without any manual clicks.

Magic Suggestions

Even without explicit rules, VeroBooks uses Magic Suggestions. The system learns from your history. If you've categorized "Starbucks" as "Meals & Entertainment" in the past, the system will highlight that account for you the next time a Starbucks transaction appears.

Reconciliation

Reconciliation ensures your VeroBooks records match your physical bank statement exactly. We recommend reconciling your primary accounts at least once a month.

  1. Go to Banking and select your account.
  2. Ensure all transactions for the month are Matched.
  3. Use the Reconcile tool to verify your ending balance against your statement.

Pro Tip: Bulk Actions

Need to clean up your feed? Use the checkboxes in the Live Feed to **Batch Delete** irrelevant transactions or **Batch Accept** multiple suggestions at once.
